BJCP Styles Test

The BJCP Styles Test will randomly choose 10 BJCP styles and ask you to choose the correct range out of a choice of 3 for each of: OG, FG, IBU, SRM, and ABV. After each question you'll be presented with the correct answer and a score. At the end of the 10 styles you'll be presented with an overall score and a listing of those styles you did well on, and those you didn't. You can choose to torture yourself more if you want to.
